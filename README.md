# Overview
MediHealth Ghana Ltd., a leading pharmaceutical distributor operating nationwide, has experienced inconsistent sales performance, with the Northern and Western Regions underperforming. Although several hypotheses were considered to explain the Western Region’s weak performance, the current dataset lacks the parameters needed to validate them. This signals the need for improved data collection and deeper analytics. Therefore, MediHealth should enhance its data tracking systems and consider hiring a dedicated data analyst to uncover key insights, streamline operations, and boost sales performance in weaker regions.
# Business Problem & Objectives
MediHealth Ghana Ltd. is experiencing inconsistent sales performance across its 16 operating regions, with significant underperformance in the Northern and Western Regions. Despite offering a wide range of pharmaceutical products, the company lacks sufficient data to understand the root causes of these regional disparities. This challenge limits MediHealth’s ability to make informed decisions, optimize its distribution strategy, and improve overall return on investment.

Therefore, the main objectives of the analysis are;

-  Identify patterns that are evident in sales across products, regions, and customer types.
   
-  Identify regions or customer types are performing well or poorly.

-  Compare product and regional performance using the. 

 -  Identify the customer type that brings in the most revenue.
 
 -  What 5 key metric should MediHealth track every month to improve performance.
 
 -  Recommend tools or dashboards to simplify reporting.

 -  Finally, explain how hiring a Data Analyst will benefit MediHealth in its operations.

## Hypothesis & Assumptions

-  It is possible that Products P003 and P007 are often purchased together, but the higher price of P003 may discourage customers from buying P007, resulting in P007’s low performance.

-  P003 may be a product that is frequently misused or overconsumed, which could explain its unusually high sales volume compared to other products.

-  Supply chain inconsistencies—such as frequent stockouts of P007 or delayed deliveries in certain regions—may also contribute to the product’s poor performance.

# Recommendations 

-  Implement Best-Seller Strategy by focusing more on Best-Selling Products.

-  Revive Regional Sales by boosting Sales in Weak Regions through promotional campaigns.

-  Strengthen Partnerships by providing after sales services to strengthen Key Customer Relationships.

 -  Monthly Performance Tracking through Dashboards and visual report.

   ## Metrics to Track Monthly

   |Metrics|Explanation |Importance|
   |-------|------------|----------|
   |Total Revenue (Month)|Total Revenue generated each month|To Monitor Growth over time|
   |Gross Profit Margin|Measures the percentage of revenue that exceeds the cost of goods sold|Indicates overall financial health and pricing efficiency|
   |Market Penetration Rate|Shows the percentage of potential customers who have purchased the product|Reveals market share and growth potential|
   |Cost per Acquisition|Total marketing spend over number of new customers acquired|Evaluates the efficiency of marketing campaigns|
   |Sales Attainment Percentage|Compares actual sales achieved against set targets|Tracks goal achievement and motivates sales teams to meet objective|

   # The Business Value of Sales Analyst

   With the help of tools like Power BI and Tableau, MediHealth needs a Sales Analyst to be able to;

  -  **Improves Sales Performance**: A sales analyst will help identifies trends, patterns, and performance gaps, helping the organization boost sales in underperforming areas.

-  **Enhances Decision-Making**: By providing data-driven insights, the analyst supports strategic decisions on pricing, marketing, distribution, and product positioning.

-  **Optimizes Sales Strategies**: Analyze customer behavior, regional performance, and product sales to recommend effective sales tactics and resource allocation.

-  **Forecasts Revenue Accurately**: By Using historical data and trends, the analyst provides reliable sales forecasts that guide budgeting and planning.

-  **Increases Operational Efficiency**: Through analysis, they uncover inefficiencies, reduce waste, and streamline sales processes for better productivity.

-  **Identifies High-Value Products and Markets**:The analyst will help the business focus on top-performing products and growing markets to maximize returns.

-  **Supports Marketing and Campaign Effectiveness**: By tracking customer acquisition cost, campaign success, and market penetration, they ensure marketing spend delivers real value.

-  **Strengthens Competitive Advantage**: A sales analyst monitors competitors and market shifts, helping the business stay ahead with proactive strategies.

-  **Improves Customer Satisfaction**: Through understanding customer trends and needs, they help tailor product offerings and improve customer experience.

-  **Drives Profitability**: Their insights directly contribute to better pricing, targeted sales efforts, and efficient resource use—ultimately increasing profit margins.

 # Future Improvement  

 Here are well-structured future improvements for your MediHealth analysis or dashboard project:

**1. Expand Data Collection**

Include additional metrics such as customer demographics, competitor presence, marketing spend, and product availability to explain performance gaps.

**2. Automate Reporting**

Build automated dashboards in Power BI to reduce manual work and ensure real-time access to insights.

**3. Integrate Market & Competitor Data**

Adding external data sources will provide richer insights into market share, pricing pressure, and regional dynamics.

**4. Improve Data Quality**

Introduce data validation rules, remove duplicates, fill missing values, and standardize formats to improve analysis accuracy.

**5. Add Predictive Analytics**

Incorporate forecasting models to predict sales, product demand, and regional performance using historical trends.

**6. Implement Customer Segmentation**

Analyze customer types, buying behavior, and retention rates to improve targeted marketing strategies.

**7. Track Operational Metrics**

Add metrics like delivery time, stock-out frequency, and supply chain efficiency to understand backend contributors to sales declines.

**8. Build Region-Level Dashboards**

Develop region-specific dashboards for Northern, Western, and other regions to help managers see challenges and opportunities clearly.
